My Books

Child of Fire

After Amita awakens a mysterious power inside herself, she must explore herself, battle her past and travel across her world to find safety. She knows how indifferent the world can be. But this time she must fight, fight for her life and her future, because nothing is as it seems...

Legacy of Air

When Kiellian finds himself the bearer of an ancient and powerful magic, he makes a bargain with a withered stranger to bring him to safety. But what Kiellian doesn't know is who he has suddenly allied with, or how far he must go to find safety...

Descendant of Earth

Ember has always known there are monsters. But as she turns sixteen, she discovers a different sort—the magical kind. On her quest to find
safety, Ember is pursued by the demons of her past, the monsters lurking in her life and the beasts of the magical world. As danger grows
nearer, there is more at stake than ever before. And Ember might find
more than just what she's looking for—a little bit of herself. 

Legacy of Water

Caspian has been trained in magic ever since he could walk—before his power was awakened. But when he leaves his island to escape his family, he discovers there's so much more out there that he's never known. Not just demons who will hunt his power to the end of the world, but the sting of betrayal... Meanwhile, the demons are getting restless, and war is on the horizon...

Only A World Away

When Sage trips into Annie at the library, neither of them expect to be
transported into another world: an alternate Melbourne that runs on not
technology, but magic.

Nine Days Until Forever

Ambrosia is trapped in an endless cycle; all she ever gets with her supposed soulmate Regan is nine days. No more, no less. She can count the seconds before the nine days are up and he slips away from her. She doesn’t know why, but she does know one thing: she needs to escape the cycle before it consumes her.

Iris has only just gotten rid of her toxic ex-boyfriend, barely days after she breaks free from her abusive father. Lost in the maze of life, Iris must slowly find her way again. And it is a slow journey; but if there is one thing she has learnt from life, it is that small steps add up.

Everything changes when Ambrosia falls from the sky into the Belowworld, her wings broken and feathers filling the air. Ambrosia and Iris both stopped believing in fate long ago, but maybe sometimes it takes more than destiny to find what you’re looking for…
